Bacalhau com Natas

Description

Cod with cream is one of the most appreciated cod recipes. Creamy and flavorful, it's a must-try dish! Learn how to make it.

Ingredients

  • Potato
  • Cod
  • Frying oil
  • BÃ©chamel sauce
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Pre-heat the oven at 220C.
  2. Cut the potatos in cubes and fry them in frying oil.
  3. In a pan add the cod and fill it with water, boil it in high heat until it starts to bubble and reduce it to low it for 5 more min.
  4. After letting the cod cool down break it up into tinny pieces.
  5. In a pan fry the onion and after add the cod and the potatos,after mixing well add 2/3 of the bechamel sause.
  6. Put the mixure in the oven after covering the top with the rest of the bechamel sause and cheese.Bake for 20min.
