Francesinha

Description

Francesinha is a portuguese dish made of all types of meats and cheese covered in a golded sauce. The assembled dish is typically baked in an oven until the cheese is melted.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Ham
  • Sausage
  • Steak
  • Cheese
  • Egg
  • Olive oil
  • Butter
  • Onion
  • Beer
  • White wine
  • English sauce
  • Tomate

Steps

  1. Toast the slices of bread and slice the sausage length wise.
  2. Season the beef and cook it along side butter.
  3. Make the sandwich and cover with cheese.
  4. Lightly cook the onion then add the sliced tomato, wine, water, beer, english sauce and let it simmer for 10min .
  5. Put 3 table spoons of sauce over the sandwich and let it broil in the oven for 5min.
  6. Put the rest of the sauce over the sadwich.
  7. Fry an egg and put it on top.
